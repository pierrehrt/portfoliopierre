The
challenge
Redesigning a mobile app and its website with a some thousands user base is always a big responsibility. Users sometimes react negatively to changes in interactions that have become familiar, which can bring down ratings. I worked with FlaminGO! Team to find the perfect concepts and solution for improving the design while keeping users comfortable with familiar interactions.
Website  &
App Design
The
mission
My mission was to refresh the brand guideline in order to bring a new, dark and modern design to the website. On the following, I had to add all the database present in the app on the website, this way even on browsers people can now discover all the places that FlaminGO! has selected for you.
